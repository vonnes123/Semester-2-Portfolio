My media production porfolio

Here are some of the things I have done

  • Photography

    While learning how to use all the different settings of a camera I was taking pictures. These pictures show all the different ways you can use a camera. I documented all my findings and put them in this portfolio created in adobe inDesign and edited them using Photoshop. In total I took around 1000 pictures and use around 100.

  • Short documentarie

    For this assignment me and my group had to create a short documentary. We chose the main topic of our documentary to be PSV since we are all currently based in Eindhoven. We used DaVinci Resolve to edit our short documentary. We went through the whole process from story boards to actual video.

  • Poster design

    Our first task for adobe InDesign was to create a poster. Sounds simple at the start, however once you start designing it you learn that there are a lot of small details that need to be just about right so that the poster looks good. Type, color, placement, white space and many other played a huge role in the design.

  • Logo design

    The first adobe program we tackled was the famous adobe illustrator. Our task was to create a logo I started out by outlining some paper sketches and then moved to digital. Sadly I was not satisfied with the first results so I did what good logo designers do and I iterated a lot until I found a design that I liked.
